Mojito

Description

Use Captain Morgan Parrot Bay Key Lime Rum to make this easy and authentic-tasting mojito.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ ounces Captain Morgan Parrot Bay Key Lime
  • 2 ½ ounces club soda
  • 2 sprigs mint
  • 1 wedge lime

Directions

  1. Muddle mint.
  2. Fill glass with ice.
  3. Add Parrot Bay Key Lime and club soda.
  4. Garnish with a lime wedge.
